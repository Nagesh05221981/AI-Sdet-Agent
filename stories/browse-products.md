# Browse Products

As a shopper, I want to land on the Eclat shop and immediately see the
catalogue so I can decide what to buy.

## Acceptance criteria

- The product grid renders on page load (at least one .pcard is visible).
- Each product card shows its name, category and price.
- The #results-info text reflects the visible product count.
- The cart starts empty (#cart-count shows 0) and #checkout-btn is disabled.
